About Me


My Hometown

I grew up in a town in the San Francisco Bay Area. My grandparents bought a house there in the 50's, which my parents bought from them in the 80's and that is the house my family continues to live in today. Danville is nestled in the golden valleys east of Oakland. The town seeks to maintain the feeling and atmosphere of a small town with few chain stores downtown and many events throughout the year. The 4th of July parade is not to be missed, and the annual Tree Lighting just after Thanksgiving brings the whole town out for the night. Sports are taken seriously there; we have some of the most competitive club and high school teams in the state. It was a great place to grow up and I would love to move back there at some point.
